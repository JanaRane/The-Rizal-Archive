Timeline

1861

Rizal was born on June 19, 1861 in Calamba, Laguna. He was the son of Teodora Alonso Realonda y Quintos and Francisco Rizal Mercado y Alejandro.

1872

Rizal witnessed the execution of the three priests, Mariano Gomez, Jose Burgos, and Jacinto Zamora.

Rizal started studying at Ateneo Municipal de Manila under Fr. Jose Bech, S.J.

1877

Rizal graduated with highest honors and received a degree in Bachelor of Arts.

Rizal enrolled in UST to study Philosophy and Letters.

1878

Rizal shifted to medicine due to his mother's failing eyesight

1882

Rizal left the country to sail to Spain and enroll at the Universidad Central de Madrid.

1884

Rizal completed his medical course in Universidad Central de Madrid and received his Licentiate in Medicine.

1885

Rizal finished his Philosophy and Letters degree in Universidad Central de Madrid and received his Degree of Licentiate in Philosophy and Letters.

Rizal enrolled in University of Paris to continue studying ophthalmology.

1886

Rizal enrolled in University of Heidelberg to continue studying ophthalmology under Dr. Otto Becker.

1887

Rizal's novel Noli Me Tangere was published.

1891

Rizal's novel El Filibusterismo was published.

1892

Rizal returned to the Philippines, and was imprisoned in Fort Santiago.

1896

Rizal was executed on December 30, 1896.
